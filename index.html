About Lost River Hearing

Music City Hearing is a mobile concierge hearing care practice serving the local community. Our founder, Michael Adam Farley, combines years of expertise with personalized care. Our services focus on premium hearing aid fitting, in-home convenience, and lifetime support for our patients. Each purchase contributes to the Farley Communication Accessibility Foundation, helping children with hearing loss.

Our Services

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you bill insurance?
Currently, we operate as a direct-pay concierge service and do not bill insurance.
Do you provide in-home visits?
Yes! Our mobile practice allows us to come to your home or office.
What brands do you offer?
We provide premium Phonak hearing aids and an optional OTC backup set for qualifying patients.
How does my purchase help children?
10% of every hearing aid sale goes to the Farley Communication Accessibility Foundation to assist children with hearing loss. "When you hear, you help them to hear."

Farley Communication Accessibility Foundation

Our foundation helps children with hearing loss access the technology and support they need. Every purchase you make helps us provide hearing aids, therapy, and education to kids who need it most.
